About history of xenix

Since 1988. Xenix Electech is a reputable local engineering firm very experienced in the supply of railway technologies for SRT over many years. Products and systems manufactured, delivered and supported by Xenix include Genertor Set, Power Supplies, Signalling Wayside Equipments, Insulated Rail Joints, Signals and Complete Level Crossing Solutions.


Also our experiences in generator industry over 30 years in Thailand, Xenix Electech plays an important role of being product quality and services provider. Xenix’s solution serving the power need of household, institution, government, industrial and residential. Our products are served to saving clients’ need which can be can be other customer built or tailor made.
